Our approach

Our simple and comprehensive 3-step approach is designed to bring you from healthy to healthier.

1

Baseline

Your body composition, lifestyle choices, and environmental factors contribute to your unique baseline health. Our Baseline programme will give you a clear understanding of your body’s current health status and inherited risks.

2

Control & Reverse

If we identify any health risk during your Baseline assessment, our team of medical specialists will create a personalised recovery plan for you as part of our Control & Reverse programme.

3

Optimise

Designed by doctors, our Optimise programme brings you from healthy to healthier. Our catalogue of physician-led programmes enhances your health by optimising your lifestyle and helping you manage chronic symptoms.

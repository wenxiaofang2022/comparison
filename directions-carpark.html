Driving?

From the Ayer Rajah Expressway (AYE), take the Normanton Park or South Buona Vista Road exit and turn into Science Park Drive carpark.

Step 1

Find a carpark lot close to ‘Retail Lift Lobby'.
Once properly parked, exit via 'Retail Lift Lobby'.

Step 2

Take the lift to level 1

Step 3

Exit from the glass door.

Step 4

You will see ‘Aglio Olio’ infront. Walk towards the direction pointed on the floor.

Step 5

Continue walking straight.

Step 6

You will see “The Good Beer Co.” walk towards the direction pointed on the floor.

Step 7

early Medical Centre is just right at the corner.

3D Automated Breast Ultrasound

Invenia ABUS

Low radiation Breast Cancer screening for Asian women under 40.

About 80 percent of Asian women have dense breast tissue. Both dense breast tissue and cancer appear white on a mammogram, creating a dangerous camouflage effect and a dilemma for radiologists whose goal is to find breast cancer as early as possible.

The addition of ABUS to your screening exam can provide a more comprehensive evaluation, help your physician identify any abnormalities in your tissue, and determine the next steps in your care.

Cancer can be hard to spot in Mammogram compared with ABUS.

The only FDA approved 3D Ultrasound specifically designed for detecting cancer in a dense breast tissue.

More comfortable than mammogram

ABUS provides a more comfortable screening experience compared with the mammogram.

The gentle shape of the Reverse Curve™ transducer follows the natural contour of the breast, providing patient comfort, full contact and helping ensure comprehensive coverage.

Low radiation with fast screening time

ABUS is a non-ionizing alternative to other supplemental screening options like mammogram. Hence, making ABUS a much less invasive Breast Cancer screening option especially for women under 40.

With its 30% faster screening time compared with handheld Ultrasound, ABUS is quick and accessible for busy women.

Accuracy in diagnosis for dense breasts

Screening mammography in women with dense breasts has reduced sensitivity. ABUS is specifically designed for detecting cancer in dense breast tissue.

Compared to mammography alone, ABUS imaging looks differently at dense breast tissue, providing a comprehensive view of the breast. This technology has demonstrated a 35.7% increase in cancer detection over mammography alone for these women.*

*FDA PMA P1 10006 summary of safetv and effectiveness.
